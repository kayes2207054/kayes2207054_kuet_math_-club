About Us

The KUET Math Club (Khulna University of Engineering & Technology) is a student-run organization fostering mathematical curiosity, critical thinking and collaboration. We host events for all levels — from friendly problem sessions to competitive training.

Mission

Promote mathematics literacy, provide mentoring, and encourage participation in national and international mathematical activities.

Vision

Build an inclusive community where KUET students grow mathematically and apply problem-solving to real-world challenges.
