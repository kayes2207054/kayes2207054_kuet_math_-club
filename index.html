Who we are

About KUET Math Club

Our Identity

We are a student-led organization at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, building an academic culture where mathematics is practical, competitive, and collaborative.

Mission

Strengthen mathematical literacy, mentor junior students, and prepare participants for olympiads, technical interviews, and advanced studies.

Vision

Create an inclusive, high-performing campus ecosystem where mathematical thinking drives innovation across departments.

What we do

Programs & Activities

📐

Olympiad Track

Structured sheets, guided solutions, and timed mock rounds for competition-readiness.

🧮

Concept Workshops

Topic-based workshops in algebra, combinatorics, number theory, and geometry.

🎤

Seminar Series

Faculty and alumni sessions on higher studies, research exposure, and career pathways.

🤝

Peer Mentoring

Open support sessions where seniors mentor juniors on strategy and fundamentals.

🏆

Campus Competitions

Inter-department and team challenges to practice under pressure and build confidence.

📚

Resource Library

Curated reading lists, archived questions, and discussion-based solution materials.
