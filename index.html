About the club

An academic community built by students, for students.

Our mission

Make advanced mathematical thinking accessible, practical, and exciting for every KUET student regardless of department or prior experience.

How we work

We combine guided problem sets, peer reviews, timed contests, and mentor feedback so members improve steadily and confidently.

What members gain

Sharper analytical ability, stronger interview readiness, collaboration skills, and a supportive network of ambitious peers.

Why join us

A growth system, not just a club membership.

  • Weekly focused training with clear progression paths
  • Mentorship from seniors and alumni with competition experience
  • Practice culture that improves confidence under pressure
  • Exposure to higher study and research opportunities

Member outcomes after one semester

  • Average 40% faster problem-solving speed in mock rounds
  • Regular participation in team contests and seminars
  • Improved communication through solution presentations

Programs

Signature activities that create measurable growth.

Olympiad Preparation Lab

Structured training for number theory, combinatorics, geometry, and algebra with weekly mock tests.

Proof and Logic Studio

Build clarity in proof-writing, mathematical argument, and presentation through guided peer sessions.

Math for Engineers Series

Applied workshops connecting core math to coding, optimization, and research problem formulation.

Mentor Hours

Open office hours where seniors mentor juniors on strategy, resources, and learning plans.

Campus Challenge League

Team-based contests designed to improve speed, accuracy, and calm decision making.

Research and Higher Study Talks

Insight sessions from faculty and alumni on research mindset, scholarships, and global opportunities.
